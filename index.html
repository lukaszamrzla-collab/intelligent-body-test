The Intelligent Body
200 Hour Yoga Teacher Training

Yoga is more than movement. It is a practice that integrates the body, the breath and the mind.

The Intelligent Body was created for those who want to teach yoga with depth, confidence and understanding. It blends modern anatomy and biomechanics with the timeless wisdom of yoga, offering a practical and thoughtful approach to both personal practice and teaching.

Throughout the training, you will learn how the body moves, how to build a safe and sustainable asana practice, and how to adapt postures to meet the needs of different individuals. Alongside this, you will explore the essential practices of pranayama, meditation and yoga philosophy, discovering how breath, awareness and movement work together to create a meaningful yoga experience.

This training is designed to help you think like a teacher rather than simply follow sequences. You will learn to observe, ask questions, make informed decisions and guide students with clarity and compassion.
